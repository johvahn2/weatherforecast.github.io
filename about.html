About Weather Forecast App

Welcome to our Weather Forecast Web Application! This project is part of our Web Technology assignment at Napier University. Our goal with this application is to provide users with convenient access to accurate and up-to-date weather forecasts for locations worldwide.

Features

  • Search Functionality: Quickly retrieve weather forecasts for specific locations.
  • Geolocation Services: Instantly view weather forecasts for your current location.
  • Favorite Locations: Save and track weather conditions for your preferred destinations.
  • Detailed Weather Data: Access detailed weather information to make informed decisions.
